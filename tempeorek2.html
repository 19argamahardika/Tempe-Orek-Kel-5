Tempe Orek

A simple yet flavorful Indonesian dish

Description

Tempe Orek is a classic Indonesian dish made from fried tempeh cooked in a sweet-savory sauce. It is a perfect side dish for rice and is enjoyed across the country.

Purpose

The goal of making Tempe Orek is to create a nutritious, tasty, and easy-to-make meal that pairs well with various Indonesian dishes.

Ingredients & Tools

  • 200g tempeh (cut into small cubes)
  • 3 shallots (thinly sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 red chili (optional, for spice)
  • Cooking oil
  • 50 ml water

How to Make Tempe Orek

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry the tempeh until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saute shallots, garlic, and chili (if using) until fragrant.
  3. Add sweet soy sauce, palm sugar, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Pour in 50 ml of water and let it simmer for a minute.
  5. Add the fried tempeh back into the pan and mix until the tempeh is well coated.
  6. Cook for another 2 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Remove from heat and serve warm with rice.

Conclusion

Tempe Orek is a simple yet delicious dish that highlights the rich flavors of Indonesian cuisine. With its easy preparation and satisfying taste, it remains a favorite for many.
